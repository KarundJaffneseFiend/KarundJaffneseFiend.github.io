About Me

Hi! I'm Karunniyan Sritharan, I'm currently a Multimedia student at NSBM.

As a lifelong etymology enthusiast, I’ve always been fascinated by how names carry stories—cultural, historical, personal. My curiosity spans across different languages, regions, and naming traditions, and I created NAMES4U as a platform to explore and share this world with others & create an even bigger database for likeminded people.

About This Website

NAMES4U is more than a design project—it's a curated experience that brings naming traditions to life through a more modern, and accessible interface.

The purpose of this website is to make global naming customs both discoverable and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you're looking to understand your own name, researching a name from another culture, or simply exploring the rich stories that names hold, this platform is built for you.

From detailed name search results and cultural breakdowns to a daily updated feed and map-based exploration, NAMES4U is a celebration of human identity through naming.

This project is a blend of my technical skills, cultural interest, and design sensibility—all driven by the joy of learning and storytelling through language.
